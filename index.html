QR generator by MissionControl

https://mcontrol.ml

This tool allows you to generate a QR code to connect your address to Terra Station Mobile, you can use it if you have a Ledger or if you prefer not to keep your private keys on your smartphone, (with this QR it will not be possible to sign transactions from mobile but only view the balance of your address)

If your phone can't scan this code, open the high contrast image
